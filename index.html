San Francisco, CA

San Francisco is the city where you embark and disembark the cruise ship. Don't forget to take a picture on the gangway!

Days at sea

Days at sea do not stop at a city. You won't reach the next port until the day. On the last day though, the waves are pretty high and the bot is a little wobbly! The pool is very fun because it is like a wave pool! Be careful though!

Santa Barbara, CA

Getting into Santa Barbra is more fun than you think! You get to ride a lifeboat to the port! The sand comes too high for the cruise ship to dock, so you get to ride a lifeboat! Pretty fun!

I have been to this spot! It is the Mission of Santa Barbara.

Long Beach, CA

Have fun! Visit Los Angeles or even some other fun places!

San Diego, CA

Visit the USS Midway and learn about the Battle of Midway and how USS Midway collided with another ship during a battle!

USS Midway

Ensenada, Mexico!

Walk around town and bring some money! The prices are very cheap here! But remember, you can't take food onto the ship.

At the little shops, you can see:

  • Little Games
  • Trinkets
  • Candy or gum
  • Clothing
And much more!
